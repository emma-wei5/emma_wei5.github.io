Separated, Approaching, and Bimodal plots Plots fitted using a polynomial equation. Images 4 and 5 show the coefficients if they were expressed as a 4 degree polynomial. Image 6 shows 10 degree polynomials overlayed. However, we can see that even as a 10 degree polynomial, the fit is quite poor. Thus, we choose to use a Gaussian function to model the data. I used a double Gaussian function, with two peaks, for each. This is our bimodal plot. The two peaks are independent, so I have expressed each peak separating, also with their respective means and variation.
